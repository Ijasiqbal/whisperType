Vozcribe for Windows

Version 1.0.0 · VozcribeSetup.exe · 64-bit

Free to try. No subscription required to get started. Install takes under a minute.

Windows will show a SmartScreen alert. We'll walk you through it.

After installing

1

Launch Vozcribe

It sits in your system tray. Click the icon to open settings or press your hotkey anywhere.

2

Sign in & pick a hotkey

Default is Ctrl + Shift. Change it any time from settings.

3

Click into any text field and speak

Hold the hotkey, talk, release. Transcribed text appears where your cursor is — Word, Slack, browsers, anywhere.

Requirements

Windows 10 (64-bit) or Windows 11
A working microphone
Internet connection for transcription
~250 MB disk space (.NET runtime bundled)

# Privacy Policy for WhisperType

**Last Updated: January 3, 2026**

## Introduction

WhisperType ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our WhisperType mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Audio Data
- **Microphone Access**: Our App requires access to your device's microphone to provide voice-to-text transcription services.
- **Audio Processing**: Audio recordings are captured temporarily and sent to our secure servers for transcription using AI technology.
- **Data Retention**: Audio data is processed in real-time and is NOT stored permanently on our servers. Audio files are deleted immediately after transcription is complete.

### Account Information
- **Google Sign-In**: We use Google Sign-In for authentication. We collect your email address and display name from your Google account.
- **Usage Data**: We track your transcription usage (minutes used) to manage free trial and subscription limits.

### Subscription Information
- **Payment Processing**: Subscription payments are processed through Google Play. We do not store your payment card information.
- **Subscription Status**: We store your subscription status and renewal dates.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide voice-to-text transcription services
- Authenticate your identity
- Manage your subscription and usage limits
- Improve our services and user experience
- Send important service-related notifications

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure cloud infrastructure
- Access controls and authentication

## Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Sign-In**: For authentication ([Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **Google Play Billing**: For subscription management ([Google Play Terms](https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/))
- **Firebase**: For authentication and backend services ([Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy))

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt out of non-essential data collection
- Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
